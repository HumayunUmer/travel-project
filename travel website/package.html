surmo broq

Surmo Broq Tour Package: 55,000 PKR

Surmo is located approximately 12 kilometres (7.5 mi) from the district administrative capital of Khaplu. Nagar is a former princely state and one of the ten districts of Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan. The valley is along the Karakoram Highway on the way north from the city of Gilgit. chorbat valley

Chourbar Valley Tour Package: 60,000 PKR

chorbat valley is a section of the Shyok river valley divided between Pakistan-administered Gilgit-Baltistan and Indian-administered Ladakh.
